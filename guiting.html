Mt. Guiting-Guiting

Mt. Guiting-Guiting, also known as the "Knife Peak," is the highest and most iconic mountain in Sibuyan, Romblon. Its jagged ridges, steep slopes, and challenging trails make it a haven for experienced mountaineers and adventurers alike. Standing at 2,058 meters above sea level, the peak offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the surrounding landscapes, including lush forests, valleys, and neighboring islands. The hike to the summit is demanding, requiring not only physical endurance but also technical climbing skills. Despite the challenges, the stunning scenery and the sense of accomplishment upon reaching the top make it one of the most rewarding hikes in the Philippines.

Why visit Mt. Guiting-Guiting?

  • It is the highest peak in Sibuyan, offering panoramic views of the island.
  • The challenging hike attracts seasoned mountaineers seeking adventure.
  • The mountain is home to diverse wildlife and rich biodiversity.
  • Its rugged terrain and jagged peaks provide a thrilling climb for thrill-seekers.
  • The sense of achievement after reaching the summit is unmatched.
  • It offers an opportunity to experience nature in its rawest and most pristine form.
