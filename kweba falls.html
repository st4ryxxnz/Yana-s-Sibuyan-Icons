Kweba Falls

Kweba Falls is a hidden waterfall located in the lush mountains of Sibuyan Island, Romblon. This natural wonder is a peaceful retreat for those looking to experience the raw beauty of nature. The falls cascade down from a steep cliff, creating crystal-clear pools that invite visitors to relax and swim. Surrounded by dense forests, the sound of the water and the rustling leaves create a serene atmosphere, making it the perfect place to unwind. The hike to the falls is equally rewarding, offering views of the island's diverse flora and fauna. If you're looking for an off-the-beaten-path destination with tranquility and natural beauty, Kweba Falls is a must-visit spot.
