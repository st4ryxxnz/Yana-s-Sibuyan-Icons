Cresta De Gallo

Cresta De Gallo, located on Sibuyan Island in Romblon, is a pristine tropical destination that captivates travelers with its untouched beauty. Known for its clear white sand, majestic rock formations and crystal-clear waters, it is a secluded paradise for those who seek adventure, relaxation, and a deep connection with nature. The island is a popular spot for divers, nature enthusiasts, and anyone looking to experience a peaceful, natural environment far from the crowds. Whether you're exploring the rugged coastlines, enjoying the panoramic views, or simply basking in the serene surroundings, Cresta De Gallo offers a unique getaway in the heart of the Philippines.

Why visit Cresta De Gallo?

  • Majestic rock formations that create stunning vistas and great photo opportunities.
  • Secluded beaches that offer a peaceful retreat away from the crowds.
  • Rich marine biodiversity, perfect for diving enthusiasts and nature lovers.
  • A great destination for those looking to unwind, relax, and connect with nature.
